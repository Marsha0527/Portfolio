Focus Forward

2020 was a year of change for everyone, myself included. I found myself out of a job, and in need of a completely new career. My husband has worked IT for years, and he gifted me some courses to try my hand at coding. In the beginning, I enjoyed watching the changes that I could make happen with a bit of code. Then as I got deeper into the courses, well it became harder, but the pure excitement felt when figuring out any issue you may be stuck on is so gratifying, it will make you smile everytime. I challenge myself daily, learning new concepts and coding tips. I am overjoyed to be on this new adventure in my life, and look forward to building many new, innovative websites.

Being able to read code, interpret its meaning, and having the ability to work up amazing web pages, gives me an incredible creative outlet.

Focus is key in keeping clean code and making the best possible outcome for clients. Striving to always make visually interesting sites.

It is intimidating to begin a new career, especially one that has so many challenges, but also it brings a great amount of rewards, and gratification.
